# 🎬 Netflix — Improving Product Adoption Rate

## Overview
A product case study analysing Netflix's challenge of low feature 
adoption among existing subscribers. Identified the core problem 
through user research, prioritised solutions using the RICE framework, 
and proposed BUZZ — a stories-based feature to drive product adoption 
among working professionals aged 24–40 in Tier 1 cities.

## Type
Product Management Case Study

## Tools and Methods
User Research | RICE Framework | Wireframing | KPI Design | Jobs To Be Done

## Key Findings
- 78% of users don't recall the latest features launched by Netflix
- Only 37% are satisfied with current feature guidance
- 50% of users are mobile-first
- 72% of paying customers come from Tier 1 cities

## Solution — BUZZ
A stories-based feature similar to Instagram Stories embedded in 
the Netflix app to communicate new features to existing users.

## RICE Prioritisation

| Solution | Reach | Impact | Confidence | Effort | Score |
|----------|-------|--------|------------|--------|-------|
| BUZZ | High (3) | High (3) | High (3) | Low (1) | **27** |
| Netflix Circles | High (3) | Medium (2) | Medium (2) | Low (1) | 12 |
| Questflix | Medium (2) | Low (1) | Low (1) | Low (1) | 2 |

## North Star Metric
Engagement Rate = (Users who interacted with BUZZ / Total users) × 100
