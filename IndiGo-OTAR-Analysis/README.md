# ✈️ IndiGo Airlines — Maximising On-Time Arrival Rate (OTAR)

## Overview
Analysis of 5.8M+ U.S. domestic flight records from 2015 to 
identify key delay factors and recommend strategies to improve 
IndiGo's OTAR from 62.5% to 80% as it enters the U.S. market.

## Tools
Python | Pandas | Matplotlib | Seaborn | Tableau | Google Colab

## Key Results
- Morning flights (5 AM) had highest OTAR at 77.6%
- Evening flights (6-8 PM) had lowest OTAR at 54.2%
- Taxi-out above 30 mins dropped OTAR below 25%
- Saturday had highest weekly OTAR at 66.4%
