# 🛍️ Zara — Maximising Gross Margin through Data-Driven Retail Strategy

## Overview
Analysis of 6.4M+ retail transactions across 35 stores and 
1.6M+ customer profiles to uncover hidden margin opportunities 
and recommend strategies to improve Zara's gross margin.

## Tools
Python | Pandas | Matplotlib | Seaborn | Tableau | Google Colab

## Key Results
- 0% discount yields highest GM at $127.88 per transaction
- 50%+ discounts drop margin by 66% to just $43.41
- Masculine category has highest GM at $125.31
- Basket size of 6+ items generates average GM of $711
- 2% GM improvement = $400M additional annual profit
