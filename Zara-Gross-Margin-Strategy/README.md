# 🛍️ Zara — Maximising Gross Margin through Data-Driven Retail Strategy

## Overview
Analysis of 6.4M+ retail transactions across 35 stores and 1.6M+ customer 
profiles to uncover hidden margin opportunities and recommend strategies 
to improve Zara's gross margin across products, discounts, and store locations.

## Tools
Python | Pandas | Matplotlib | Seaborn | Tableau | Google Colab

## Approach
- Defined Gross Margin = Revenue − Production Cost as the core KPI
- Analyzed 6 datasets: transactions, customers, products, stores, employees, discounts
- Performed EDA across discount bands, product categories, seasonal trends and store performance
- Built ZDMP (Zara Discount and Margin Protection Protocol) — a framework of 
  actionable rules to protect and grow margin without hurting sales volume
- Validated hypotheses on discount depth, basket size, store-level pricing and return impact

## Key Results
- 0% discount yields highest GM at $127.88 per transaction
- 50%+ discounts drop GM by 66% to just $43.41
- Masculine category has highest GM at $125.31 vs Children at $78.17
- Basket size of 6+ items generates average GM of $711 vs $98 for single items
- High-discount stores (>13%) yield margins below $30 vs $350 for controlled stores
- ZDMP implementation could prevent $10–15M in annual margin leakage
- 2% GM improvement = $400M additional annual profit for Zara
